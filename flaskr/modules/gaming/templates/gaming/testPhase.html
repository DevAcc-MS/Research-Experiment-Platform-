Part #2: Test Phase

There would be 20 tasks in the test phase. In each task you will be taken to a source object position and will be asked to locate the ‘instructed’ destination object, followed by virtually walking to the destination object.

The information of source & 'instructed' destination object, & other instructions would be displayed on screen as shown below:

In each task, you'll be instructed to point from source object (here, it's Gear) towards a destination object (here, it's Earth). You'll be then asked to virtually walk to its location. When you are ready press R.

You'll have 10 seconds to orient yourself in the virtual environment, after which walls & objects will disappear.

Now orient yourself towards the location of destination object (here Snowman), and then Press T to register the pointing direction

Now, walk towards the destination object's (here, Snowman) location. Once you think you have reached its location, press 'C'
