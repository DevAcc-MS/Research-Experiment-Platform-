Cognitive Science Lab, IIIT-H
Welcomes you for this research

This experiment is conducted to assess the spatial knowledge acquisition while navigating in virtual maze environment.
       This experiment is approximately half an hour long. The experiment has three phases: exploration phases, test phase, and self-report phase.

  • Exploration phase: you will be required to explore the environment for 10 minutes. The window will close after completing 10 minutes of exploration.
  • Test Phase: You will be taken to a start object position and will be asked to locate the ‘instructed’ target object, followed by virtually walking to the target object.
  • Self-report Questionnaires: You will be asked to fill certain details relevant to the study.
